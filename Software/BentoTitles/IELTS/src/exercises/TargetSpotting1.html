Gossip...

'Hello, Sufiya?' It was Shama Shakil on the phone.

'Shama, how are you, what's up?'

'Have you heard about Myna Hyder?' asked Shama. 'Her husband has left her! He just walked out of the house on Saturday afternoon. He said that he had never loved her and that he was going to make a new life for himself!'

'How awful! How is poor Myna?'

'Well,' said Shama, 'Naturally, she's very upset. She said she had simply not suspected anything was wrong. Then suddenly he was off down the steps with his suitcase shouting "That's it! I'm leaving! I told you to stop feeding me that banana and garlic curry!"

'What?' exclaimed Sufiya. 'He said he was leaving her because of her curry?'

'Incredible, isn't it?' said Shama with satisfaction. 'And Myna was always talking about how much Raz loved her cooking'

Sufiya wondered if there was anything they could do for Myna. She also wondered about how Myna would manage without her husband.
