How to do the exercises

Stopgap / Cloze Click on the underlined gaps and type your answer. When you have finished, either press Enter to go to the next gap, or click on another gap with the mouse.
Multiple Choice / Quiz / Analyze Click on the options next to a question to choose your answer. Click on any other option in the same question to change your mind.
Dropdown Click on the underlined gap (next to where is a little arrow). Select the correct answer from the list.
Proof Reading / Target Spotting Click on any word(s) that you think are wrong. If you click in a place where there is no mistake you will lose marks.
Countdown Click in the box at the top of the screen and type a word that you think is in the text. Press Enter or click "Guess". If you are right, the word will appear in the text everywhere that it is used. The statistics list on the right will show you how many times the word was used.
Drag and Drop / DragOn Click on one of the words that the exercise says to drag, keep the mouse down and drag to a gap. When the mouse cursor is over the gap let go. You can drag a word many times so if you change your mind, just drag another word over the wrong one.

Click marking when you have finished the exercise to see your score.

Options

Instant/delayed marking

In some exercises you will see " instant" or "delayed" next to the marking button. Click to change the type of marking that you get. With instant marking, your first choice is the one that sets your score. With delayed marking, your score is set when you click marking and not before.

Hints

In some exercises you can get a hint by holding down the CTRL key when you click on an answer or a gap.

Feedback

In some exercises you can see feedback after marking by clicking the feedback button.

Dictionary

If you hold down the CTRL key and click on any word in an exercise, you will be linked to an online dictionary to look up that word.

Sound control

When a sound, animation or video is playing you can pause or rewind it by clicking on the media controller on the screen. This controls whichever is the latest media you have clicked on or the exercise has played.
 

Features

Scratch Pad

This is a mini-notepad that will be saved for you between uses. You can type in it or cut and paste things. You can print it out or email (if this is setup on your webserver) it to someone. So long as you use the same login details each time you use Author Plus, it will remember everything that you type

Progress

So long as you use the same login details each time you use Author Plus, it will remember all your scores. You can get a list of all your scores by clicking the progress button. The menus also show you how much of a unit you have done with the little pie-chart in the corner of each menu.

Performance

The first time that you run Author Plus you will have to wait for a few minutes for the program to download. But after this you will be able to run quickly. This is because your browser stores the programs in its memory. Some time later you might clear out your memory and after that you will again get a slow download for one time.
