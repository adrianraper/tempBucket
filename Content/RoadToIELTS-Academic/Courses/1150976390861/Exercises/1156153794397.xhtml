What will I learn in this unit?

General introduction to the speaking test.

You will ...

  1. 1. refresh key points and timing for the speaking test.
  2. 2. watch video clips of a candidate and the examiner starting the speaking test.
  3. 3. watch candidates describing their home towns.

The speaking test is a one-to-one interview which assesses how well you can communicate in English.

The interview lasts for 11-14 minutes and has three parts.

Part 1: You answer general questions about yourself, your life and interests.
