What will I learn in this unit?

Advantages and disadvantages You will ...

  1. 1. analyse a writing task and then listen to students discussing the task.
  2. 2. organise notes into paragraphs and group linking words and phrases according to their function.
  3. 3. complete a composition with appropriate linking words and phrases.
  4. 4. group adjectives according to how the comparative is formed.
  5. 5. complete sentences using comparative structures.
  6. 6. write a composition discussing advantages and disadvantages.
