What will I learn in this unit?

Leisure activities

You will ...

  1. 1. listen to people talking about how they spend their free time and decide what their leisure activities are.
  2. 2. read an email describing how someone spends their free time and complete it with appropriate words and phrases.

Sports, games and equipment

You will ...

  1. 1. match the names of sports and games to pictures.
  2. 2. match equipment to sports and games.
  3. 3. do a quiz about sports and games.
