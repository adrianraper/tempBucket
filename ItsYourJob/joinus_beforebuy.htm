Important:

When the PayDollar website is loading, do not click any buttons on the navigation bar of your browser.

You must activate Verified by Visa if you are using Visa

What is Verified by Visa?

Verified by Visa, introduced by Visa International, is a secure and easy-to-use service which gives you extra protection when you pay online.

What should I do if I don't have Verified by Visa?

  • Please click below to go to your Card Issuer's Verified by Visa secure registration web page. If you don't see your bank or organisation, please contact it directly.
    - Asia
    - Europe
  • Enter your existing Visa Card details as requested.
  • Create your personal message (PAM) and personal password (once registered, you can use this password each time you make a purchase).
  • Purchase subscription to It's Your Job with Verified by Visa.

You must enter SecureCode if you are using Mastercard

What is SecureCode?

MasterCard SecureCode is a global e-commerce solution, enabling cardholders to authenticate themselves to their issuer with a unique, personal code.

What should I do if I don't have SecureCode?

  • Go to your Card Issuer's website and register for the SecureCode service.
  • Enter your existing MasterCard details as requested.
  • Create your personal message (PAM) and password (once registered, you can use this password each time you make a purchase).
  • Issuer confirms registration
  • Purchase subscription to It's Your Job with SecureCode.
